About Us

MAHWI TECH Ltd is a Rwandan-based digital innovation company transforming agriculture through powerful ICT tools. Since 2011, we've helped 21,000+ farmers across 15 districts increase productivity, income, and sustainability through our flagship platform: M-Lima.

Mission

To empower farmers and agri-cooperatives with innovative ICT solutions that improve productivity, profitability, and sustainability.

Vision

To be Africa’s leading digital enabler of inclusive, sustainable agriculture transformation.

Our Product

  • 📱 Mobile & Web-Based Farm Management Tools
  • 📊 Real-Time Market Access & Digital Trade
  • 🤝 Financing Connections for Smallholder Farmers
  • 🌾 Extension & Training Services for Better Yields
